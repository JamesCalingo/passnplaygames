Mafia

Welcome to a lovely town...with a small probem: People keep getting killed during the night. Apparently, this town has a mafia problem...and you're stepping right into it.

Most of you will join the civilian ranks, trying to find and run those notorious mafia folks out of town. Some, however, will join the ranks of the mafia, killing civilians at night and attempting to sow discord amongst the townspeople so that they can take over.

Will the mafia take over this small village, or will the town get justice?
